9 Supporting Trans & Gender Nonconforming Students 4:00 - 5:00PM PDT. According to GLSEN research, transgender, nonbinary and gender nonconforming students face more hostile school climates than other members of the LGBTQ community. In this training, we will share statistics on national data around LGBTQ student experiences, paying specific attention to the experiences of trans* students. Educators will leave with a better understanding of LGBTQ and transgender student experiences, along with resources such as the Trans model policy. They will be given guidance and best practices on supporting transgender, nonbinary and gender nonconforming students of all age groups.

14 Real Talk - QTBIPOC Support Group 6:00 - 8:00PM PST. *REAL TALK is still operating virtually through Zoom* REAL TALK is a support/social group for Queer and/or Trans, Gender diverse, Two-Spirit, Black, Indigenous, People of Color. Here QTBIPOC individuals will engage in meaningful dialogue that will promote self love, self acceptance, safety and support. Our goal is to empower and equip our participants with the tools and confidence they need to live their authentic truths. We will build a home away from home where all walks of life can feel loved and accepted. Discussions include topics such as: * Self Care and Safety * Empowering * Self-Affirmation * Love and Acceptance * Surviving the Spaces Around Us! We want to make this and any UTOPIA future events accessible as possible. Please let us know your access needs via email at support@utopiawa.org.UTOPIA works to provide sacred spaces to strengthen the minds and bodies of QTPI -Queer and Trans Pacific Islanders- through community organizing, community care, civic engagement, and cultural stewardship. Questions or for more information please email support@utopiawa.org or call 253-478-3941.
